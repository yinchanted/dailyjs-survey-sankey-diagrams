This visualization shows how answers from one question map to another question. It allows to find interesting correlations. To get started check out the "examples" section bellow.

About

This small project was made with D3.js, Sankey plugin, Bootstrap and Flat-UI.

Data comes from Sharepoint VTC TechDay Pre-Registration Form by Yin Xu (Pre Registration, Morning Attendance, Afternoon Attendance). Single choice questions are marked with [.] and multiple choice questions with [*].
